Cycle the Continental Divide

  • When: Saturday October 6th, 2018
  • Time: 10:00 A.M.
  • Duration: One Month
  • Length: 2,768 Miles
  • Skill Level: Advanced
  • Start: Banff, Alberta CA
  • End:Antelope Wells NM
  • Price: $525.00 (per person)

The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route (GDMBR) is Adventure Cycling's premier off‐pavement cycling route, crisscrossing the Continental Divide north to south, This route is defined by the word "remote." Its remoteness equates with spectacular terrain and scenery. The entire route is basically dirt‐road and mountain‐pass riding every day. In total, it has over 200,000 feet of elevation gain.

Mystic Mountain Bike Race

  • Length. 40 Miles
  • When: Saturday July 21st, 2018
  • Time: 7:30 A.M. to 4:00 P.M.
  • Where: 3394 Bear Canyon Rd, Bozeman, MT 59715

The Mystic MTB Trail Race presented by Owenhouse Cycling is the only race of its kind in the Bozeman area. Comprised of some of the most exciting sections of remote trail in the Gallatin National Forest, the Mystic MTB Trail Race is a challenging course that takes riders over ridgelines, past alpine lakes and down flowing drainages while exploring 40+ miles of remote connected trails.
